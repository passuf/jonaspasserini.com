About

I studied computer science at ETH Zurich and specialized in Information Security during my master studies.

Currently, I work as a Junior Security Engineer at Futurae, an ETH Spin-off where we build secure and usable two-factor authentication. At SIB I teach the IT-Security lecture as part of their Chief Digital Officer postgraduate studies.

Before, I worked as a Web Engineer at UniversitätsSpital Zürich and ABZ and was the webmaster for bauenschweiz.

I love to play the clarinet, swim in the Zürichsee, read books or play Dungeons & Dragons with friends.
